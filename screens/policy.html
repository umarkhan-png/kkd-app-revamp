Policies

Privacy policy

Last updated · 1 June 2026

Katyayani Krishi Direct ("KKD") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Your name, mobile number and delivery addresses.
  • Your crops, farm size and orders to personalise advice and offers.
  • App usage to improve reliability — never your contacts or media without permission.

How we use it

To process orders, deliver products, share spray/weather alerts, and send order and offer updates over SMS/WhatsApp (with your consent given at sign-up).

Your choices

You can turn off promotional messages any time from Profile → App settings, and request deletion of your account by contacting support.

Terms of service

Last updated · 1 June 2026

By using the KKD app you agree to these terms.

  • Products are sold for agricultural use only — follow the label dose and safety instructions.
  • Prices, offers and Krishi Coins value may change without notice.
  • Delivery timelines are estimates and may vary by location and weather.
  • Misuse, fraud or reselling may lead to account suspension.

Krishi Coins

Coins are a loyalty reward (1 coin = ₹1 toward a future order), are non-transferable, non-withdrawable as cash, and may expire if the account is inactive.

Returns & refund policy

Last updated · 1 June 2026

We want you to farm with confidence. Most products carry a 7-day return from delivery.

Eligible

  • Damaged, leaking or wrong product delivered.
  • Unopened, sealed packs in original condition.
  • Shoot a short unboxing video to fast-track approval.

Not eligible

  • Opened agro-chemicals (unless damaged or incorrect).
  • Requests raised after 7 days of delivery.

Refunds

After pickup & quality check, refunds reach your original payment method or Krishi Coins wallet within 3–5 working days. Reverse pickup is free. You can choose a replacement instead of a refund.
